A formative note from a Principal visit

After spending time with the Principal — a school walk, a leadership meeting, a parent event — record what you saw across 8 focus areas. This builds the evidence base for the yearly appraisal.

  1. Rate 8 focus areas as Emerging, Developing, or Notable
  2. Add short comments — be specific and kind
  3. Submit — the note locks, so re-read first
⚠️ Observation is about growth, not grading. There is no number score here — aim for at least one observation per term.
