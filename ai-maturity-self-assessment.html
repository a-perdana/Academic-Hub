Only the School Principal, Academic Coordinator, or Foundation Representative can fill in this self-assessment. Read access is open to all school leadership.

How this works

Rate where the school is now on each of the 6 areas. Then pick a target level for next year. Add 2-4 pieces of evidence per area. List strengths and improvement areas. Save anytime. Submit when ready. After submission, ratings lock until the appraisal team visits.

Most Eduversal schools in 2026 are honestly at Step 1 or 2 on most areas. That is normal. Inflated self-assessment is a network-wide red line — the Eduversal team will see it, and the school will set goals it cannot reach.
