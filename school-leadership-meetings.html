The backend for this page is already live — Firestore collection school_leadership_meetings + school_leadership_meeting_items, rules, storage paths, and page-access gating all in place since 2026-05-21. Only the UI shell is still pending.

This page mirrors the Central Hub Coordinators workspace pattern but school-scoped: every record carries a required schoolId and the rules enforce same-school visibility via isAHSchoolLeader(schoolId). Foundation Rep + School Principal + Academic Coordinator + Cambridge Coordinator at your school can read and write; academic_admin bypasses.

What this page will do

Two-column layout: left sidebar lists meetings (filtered to your school by schoolId); right side is the active meeting's agenda-item editor. Agenda items support tags (decision/action/discussion/announcement/red_text), rich body, sub-items, subject scope. Red-text items promote into Decisions; action items spawn cards in school-scoped Activities. Collaborative editing — any school leader can amend; admin can delete.
