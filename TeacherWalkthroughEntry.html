Short, frequent classroom-visit notes

Walkthroughs are quick (a few minutes), frequent, and lower-stakes than a full appraisal. Jot what you saw — these notes build a fair picture over time and become your evidence base.

  1. Visit a classroom for a few minutes
  2. Note the focus + quick observations while it is fresh
  3. Repeat often — several across a term beats one big push
⚠️Little and often beats one rushed note at year end. Your walkthrough notes feed the formal Teacher Appraisal.
For: Academic Coordinator (+ SP, CC) Scope: Your school's teachers Stakes: Low / formative
