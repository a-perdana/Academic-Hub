School Leadership Track — browse + self-assess

Six domains anchored in Cambridge School Leader Standards 2023. Tap any competency to open it and rate yourself on each level's statements — Awareness, Practitioner, Advanced, Lead.

  1. Self-assess here — rate yourself 1–4 on each statement. Scores stay private, feed the "Avg Self-Assessment" tile above
  2. Submit evidence in My Portfolio when you're ready to claim a level officially (initiatives led, decisions made, outcomes observed)
  3. HQ reviews your submission → approved level lands on your Learning Path + Certificates
⚠️ Self-assessment alone is not a certificate. It's an honest snapshot for your own reflection — earning a level requires evidence + HQ approval.
Privacy: your SA scores are yours alone; HQ never sees them Tip: rate yourself honestly — over-rating yourself today means harder evidence to submit later
