Competency Levels
Awareness
Understands the standard and its rationale; participates in relevant processes when directed. (Cambridge School Leader Standard: beginning)
Practitioner
Applies the competency consistently at team or department level; can evidence impact on teaching and learning. (Cambridge School Leader Standard: developing)
Advanced
Leads programme or phase-level practice with critical reflection; uses evidence to drive improvement. (Cambridge School Leader Standard: established)
Lead / Mentor
Shapes whole-school systems; mentors other leaders and contributes to the Cambridge schools network. (Cambridge School Leader Standard: exemplary)

Framework Sources & References

This framework is grounded in the following internationally recognised standards and Indonesian national regulations:

📘
Cambridge School Leader Standards 2019
University of Cambridge Local Examinations Syndicate (UCLES). Defines five domains for effective school leaders in Cambridge-accredited schools: strategic direction, learning and teaching, developing people, working in partnership, and professional responsibility. Provides the primary structure for all six leadership domains in this framework.
cambridge.org/elt → Cambridge School Leader Standards ↗
🇺🇸
Professional Standards for Educational Leaders (PSEL) 2015
National Policy Board for Educational Administration (NPBEA), USA. Ten standards for school leadership excellence covering mission & vision, ethics, equity, curriculum, communities of care, professional capacity, operations, and governance. Used to supplement Cambridge standards in the People Development and Equity domains.
npbea.org → PSEL 2015 ↗
🇮🇩
Permendiknas No. 13 Tahun 2007 — Standar Kepala Sekolah/Madrasah
Kementerian Pendidikan Nasional Republik Indonesia. Establishes the five core competencies for Indonesian school principals (Kepribadian, Manajerial, Kewirausahaan, Supervisi, Sosial). Integrated into the Ethics, Accountability & Operations and Family, Community & External Partnerships domains.
peraturan.bpk.go.id → Permendiknas No. 13/2007 ↗
