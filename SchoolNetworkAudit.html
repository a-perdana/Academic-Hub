📋 About this assessment
Network tests use dual HTTP probes (fetch + image) with adaptive timeout calibrated to your connection speed. Results indicate whether sites are reachable from this browser — a blocked result is strong evidence of filtering, but not a guarantee. Items marked ⚠ low confidence should be verified manually. Device management items are self-reported — verify against your MDM/Admin Console. This tool does not perform DNS resolution bypass testing or SSL inspection.
